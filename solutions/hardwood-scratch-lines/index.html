Problem

Hardwood scratch lines remain

Straight scratch lines remain visible on hardwood.

wood scratch removal 120 → 150 → 180 → 220 hardwood scratch lines remain straight scratch lines remain visible on hardwood. wood straight scratch lines remain visible on hardwood how to fix hardwood scratch lines remain scratches too deep
Answer

120 or 150 to remove deeper lines, then 180 and 220. Sand with the grain where possible.

Why it happens
Hardwood can hold visible scratches if the previous grit pattern is not fully refined.
Recommended grit
120 or 150 to remove deeper lines, then 180 and 220.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The old marks are replaced by one uniform finer scratch pattern.

What to do

  1. Sand with the grain where possible.
  2. Use the grit that removes the visible issue.
  3. Step finer gradually.
  4. Remove dust and inspect under good light.
Avoid: Do not skip grit steps while visible scratches or high spots remain.
