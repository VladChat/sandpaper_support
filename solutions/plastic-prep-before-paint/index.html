Plastic prep before paint

Plastic needs light sanding before coating.

Solution

Problem
Plastic needs light sanding before coating.
Likely cause
Plastic can scratch or heat easily if the grit or pressure is too aggressive.
Recommended grit
320 to 600 depending on coating instructions and surface condition.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on the coating stage.
Success check
The surface is evenly dulled without deep scratches.

Steps

  1. Clean the plastic.
  2. Use light pressure.
  3. Create a uniform fine scratch pattern.
  4. Remove residue before coating.
Avoid: Do not gouge plastic with coarse grit unless heavy shaping is intentional.
