Problem

Paper tears on sharp edges

The sheet catches and rips on an edge or corner.

wet sanding dry sanding pressure
Answer

Use the correct task grit, but reduce pressure near edges. Inspect the edge.

Why it happens
Sharp edges cut into the backing under pressure.
Recommended grit
Use the correct task grit, but reduce pressure near edges.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on the task.
Success check
The sheet glides without catching.

What to do

  1. Inspect the edge.
  2. Use lighter pressure.
  3. Sand off the edge carefully instead of dragging into it.
  4. Use a block where it helps control pressure.
Avoid: Do not pull the sheet hard across sharp corners.
