Patchy surface after grit change

The surface looks uneven after switching grits.

Solution

Problem
The surface looks uneven after switching grits.
Likely cause
The previous grit pattern was not fully removed across the whole surface.
Recommended grit
Return to the previous grit or current grit until uniform.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on stage.
Success check
The whole surface changes to the same finer pattern.

Steps

  1. Inspect the entire area.
  2. Sand until the older pattern is gone.
  3. Clean before changing grits.
  4. Use the next grit evenly.
Avoid: Do not move finer while old scratches remain in spots.
