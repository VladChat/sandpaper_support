Problem

Finish smears while sanding

The finish gums up instead of sanding to powder.

Quick Answer

Use fine prep grits such as 320 to 600 when the finish is ready. Stop and clean the sheet.

Best Grit Path

320 → 600

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Stop and clean the sheet.
  2. Allow more cure time if the coating is soft.
  3. Resume with light pressure.
  4. Use fresh paper if loading continues.
Why It Happens

The finish may be too soft for sanding or too much heat is being created.

Wet or Dry

Light dry sanding or wet sanding where the finish and stage allow.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not grind soft finish with heavy pressure.
Success Check

The finish sands evenly instead of smearing.

Related Problems
