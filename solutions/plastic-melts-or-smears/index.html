Plastic melts or smears

Plastic smears, melts, or loads the sheet while sanding.

Solution

Problem
Plastic smears, melts, or loads the sheet while sanding.
Likely cause
Heat from pressure, speed, or a loaded sheet can soften plastic.
Recommended grit
Use 320 to 600 for prep, then finer grits as needed.
Wet or dry
Wet for fine stages where suitable; dry with light pressure for prep.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

Steps

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
