Problem

100 grit leaves marks before 180

100 grit scratches are still visible after moving to 180.

general sandpaper use scratch removal 100 → 120 → 150 → 180 100 grit leaves marks before 180 100 grit scratches are still visible after moving to 180. general sandpaper use 100 grit scratches are still visible after moving to 180 how to fix 100 grit leaves marks before 180 scratches too deep
Answer

Use 120 or 150 before 180 if the 100-grit marks are deep. Clean the surface.

Why it happens
The 100-grit scratch pattern was not fully refined before switching or pressure was too heavy.
Recommended grit
Use 120 or 150 before 180 if the 100-grit marks are deep.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The old marks are replaced by one uniform finer scratch pattern.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface.
  2. Use the recommended grit with light, even pressure.
  3. Inspect before changing grits.
  4. Move finer only after the old marks are gone.
Avoid: Do not skip grit steps while visible scratches or high spots remain.
