Problem

Rust pits remain

Dark pits or small low spots remain after rust sanding.

metal surface refinement 80 → 180 → 220 → 320 rust pits remain dark pits or small low spots remain after rust sanding. metal dark pits or small low spots remain after rust sanding how to fix rust pits remain surface still feels rough
Answer

Use 80 to 180 for rough cleanup, then 220 and 320 for prep. Clean the surface.

Why it happens
Pitting is below the surrounding surface, so sanding the top surface may not fully erase it.
Recommended grit
Use 80 to 180 for rough cleanup, then 220 and 320 for prep.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface.
  2. Use the recommended grit with light, even pressure.
  3. Inspect before changing grits.
  4. Move finer only after the old marks are gone.
Avoid: Do not skip grit steps while visible scratches or high spots remain.
