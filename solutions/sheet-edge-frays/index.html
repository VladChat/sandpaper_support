Problem

Sheet edge frays during sanding

The edge of the sandpaper frays and leaves fibers or rough backing.

Answer

Use the same working grit, with lighter pressure near edges. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Why it happens
The sheet edge is rubbing against corners, burrs, or a sharp surface under pressure.
Recommended grit
Use the same working grit, with lighter pressure near edges.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on the task.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
