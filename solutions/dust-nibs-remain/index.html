Problem

Dust nibs remain after sanding

Small raised dust spots remain in paint or clear coat.

Answer

Use 1000 to 2000 for small nibs, then 3000 if polishing prep is needed. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Why it happens
The nibs were not leveled before moving to finer grits.
Recommended grit
Use 1000 to 2000 for small nibs, then 3000 if polishing prep is needed.
Wet or dry
Wet for fine coating work.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not expect sanding alone to create full gloss.
