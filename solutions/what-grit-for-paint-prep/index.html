Problem

What grit for paint prep

Paint needs prep sanding before another coat.

Quick Answer

220 to 400 depending on the stage and coating instructions. Let the coating cure as directed.

Best Grit Path

220 → 400

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Let the coating cure as directed.
  2. Use light pressure.
  3. Sand only enough to smooth and prepare.
  4. Clean residue before recoating.
Why It Happens

Paint prep needs enough tooth without deep scratches.

Wet or Dry

Dry or wet depending on coating stage.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not use heavy-removal grit between finish coats.
Success Check

The surface is smooth, clean, and not cut through.
