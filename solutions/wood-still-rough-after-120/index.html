Problem

Wood still rough after 120 grit

Wood fibers or roughness remain after sanding with 120 grit.

Answer

Use 150 or 180, then 220 for smoother prep. Sand evenly with 150 or 180.

Why it happens
120 grit may still be an early prep step and needs follow-up refinement.
Recommended grit
150 or 180, then 220 for smoother prep.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The wood feels consistently smooth before finishing.

What to do

  1. Sand evenly with 150 or 180.
  2. Clean dust.
  3. Finish prep with 220 if needed.
  4. Check by touch and light.
Avoid: Do not jump from rough 120 grit directly to very fine grit.

Ask a follow-up

Ask about this answer, the next grit, or wet/dry use.
