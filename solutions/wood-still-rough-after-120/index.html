Problem

Wood still rough after 120 grit

Wood fibers or roughness remain after sanding with 120 grit.

Quick Answer

150 or 180, then 220 for smoother prep. Sand evenly with 150 or 180.

Best Grit Path

150 → 180 → 220 → 120

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Sand evenly with 150 or 180.
  2. Clean dust.
  3. Finish prep with 220 if needed.
  4. Check by touch and light.
Why It Happens

120 grit may still be an early prep step and needs follow-up refinement.

Wet or Dry

Dry.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not jump from rough 120 grit directly to very fine grit.
Success Check

The wood feels consistently smooth before finishing.
