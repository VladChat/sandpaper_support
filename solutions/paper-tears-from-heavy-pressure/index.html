Problem

Paper tears from heavy pressure

The sheet wears through or tears during sanding.

general sandpaper use sheet handling paper tears from heavy pressure the sheet wears through or tears during sanding. general sandpaper use sheet wears through or tears during sanding how to fix paper tears from heavy pressure paper tears early
Answer

Use a grit coarse enough to cut without forcing it. Reduce pressure.

Why it happens
Excess pressure creates heat and stress on the backing.
Recommended grit
Use a grit coarse enough to cut without forcing it.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on the task.
Success check
Cutting improves without tearing the sheet.

What to do

  1. Reduce pressure.
  2. Let the abrasive do the cutting.
  3. Switch to a coarser grit if removal is too slow.
  4. Replace worn sheets.
Avoid: Do not use pressure to make a too-fine grit remove material.
