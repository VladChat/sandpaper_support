Problem

1200 grit still leaves haze

The surface still has a cloudy haze after 1200 grit.

automotive / clear coat wet sanding refinement 1200 → 1500 → 2000 → 3000 1200 grit still leaves haze the surface still has a cloudy haze after 1200 grit. automotive / clear coat surface still has a cloudy haze after 1200 grit how to fix 1200 grit still leaves haze wet sanding leaves haze
Answer

1500, 2000, then 3000 as needed. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Why it happens
1200 grit can still leave visible sanding haze and may need higher grits for polishing prep.
Recommended grit
1500, 2000, then 3000 as needed.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The haze is even and ready for the next refinement or polishing step.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not expect sanding alone to create full gloss.
