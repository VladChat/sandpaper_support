Problem

Rough spots remain after fine grit

Most of the surface is smooth but some spots remain rough.

general sandpaper use surface refinement rough spots remain after fine grit most of the surface is smooth but some spots remain rough. general sandpaper use most of the surface is smooth but some spots remain rough how to fix rough spots remain after fine grit surface still feels rough
Answer

Return to the last grit that can remove the roughness. Mark the rough spots visually if needed.

Why it happens
The rough spots were not leveled before moving to a finer grit.
Recommended grit
Return to the last grit that can remove the roughness.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on the project stage.
Success check
The surface feels consistent from edge to edge.

What to do

  1. Mark the rough spots visually if needed.
  2. Sand those areas evenly with the working grit.
  3. Blend into the surrounding area.
  4. Move finer only after the spots are gone.
Avoid: Do not overwork one tiny spot and create a low area.
