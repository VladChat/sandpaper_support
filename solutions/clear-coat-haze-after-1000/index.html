Problem

Clear coat haze after 1000 grit

1000 grit wet sanding leaves a dull clear coat.

Quick Answer

1500, then 2000 and 3000 before polishing. Rinse the area.

Best Grit Path

1500 → 2000 → 3000 → 1000

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Rinse the area.
  2. Refine with 1500 grit.
  3. Continue to 2000 and 3000 if needed.
  4. Polish when the sanding haze is fine and uniform.
Why It Happens

1000 grit is often an intermediate leveling step, not the final gloss stage.

Wet or Dry

Wet.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not stop at 1000 grit when a gloss finish is expected.
Success Check

The haze becomes finer and more uniform after each grit.
