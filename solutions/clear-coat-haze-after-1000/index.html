Problem

Clear coat haze after 1000 grit

1000 grit wet sanding leaves a dull clear coat.

automotive / clear coat wet sanding refinement 1000 → 1500 → 2000 → 3000 clear coat haze after 1000 grit 1000 grit wet sanding leaves a dull clear coat. automotive / clear coat 1000 grit wet sanding leaves a dull clear coat how to fix clear coat haze after 1000 grit wet sanding leaves haze
Answer

1500, then 2000 and 3000 before polishing. Rinse the area.

Why it happens
1000 grit is often an intermediate leveling step, not the final gloss stage.
Recommended grit
1500, then 2000 and 3000 before polishing.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The haze becomes finer and more uniform after each grit.

What to do

  1. Rinse the area.
  2. Refine with 1500 grit.
  3. Continue to 2000 and 3000 if needed.
  4. Polish when the sanding haze is fine and uniform.
Avoid: Do not stop at 1000 grit when a gloss finish is expected.
