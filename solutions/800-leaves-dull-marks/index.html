800 grit leaves dull marks

800 grit leaves a dull, visible sanding pattern.

Solution

Problem
800 grit leaves a dull, visible sanding pattern.
Likely cause
800 grit is fine, but it still leaves a sanding haze that needs finer grits for gloss preparation.
Recommended grit
Move to 1000, 1500, 2000, and 3000 as needed.
Wet or dry
Wet for fine finish stages.
Success check
The haze is even and ready for the next refinement or polishing step.

Steps

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not expect sanding alone to create full gloss.
