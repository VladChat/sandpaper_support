Problem

What grit for rough spots in garage epoxy?

Garage epoxy floors can have rough high spots, bumps, or uneven texture.

drywall patch dry sanding between coats coarse grit
Answer

For rough garage epoxy spots, use 120 or 180 to level, then 220 before recoating.

Why it happens
Raised epoxy defects need leveling before recoating or topcoat blending.
Recommended grit
Use 120 or 180 for raised rough spots, then 220 for smoother prep.
Wet or dry
Dry with dust control.
Success check
The rough spot is level and clean before the next coating step.

What to do

  1. Confirm the epoxy is fully cured.
  2. Knock down raised rough spots with 120 or 180.
  3. Refine the repair with 220.
  4. Vacuum and clean dust before recoating.
Avoid: Do not sand sticky or uncured epoxy.
