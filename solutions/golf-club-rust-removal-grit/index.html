Problem

What grit for golf club rust?

Golf club heads or shafts can show small rust spots.

metal dry sanding polishing prep scratches
Answer

For golf club light rust, use 400 or 600, then 800 for finer blending.

Why it happens
Light rust needs fine abrasion so the club is cleaned without deep scratches.
Recommended grit
Use 400 or 600 for light rust, then 800 for finer blending.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
Rust is reduced without deep sanding scratches.

What to do

  1. Clean dirt and grass residue first.
  2. Sand light rust with 400 or 600.
  3. Blend with 800 if the surface is visible.
  4. Clean residue after sanding.
Avoid: Do not use coarse grit on polished or plated club surfaces.
