Problem

Sheet becomes soft after soaking too long

The sheet feels weak or soft after sitting in water.

general sandpaper use sheet handling sheet becomes soft after soaking too long the sheet feels weak or soft after sitting in water. general sandpaper use sheet feels weak or soft after sitting in water how to fix sheet becomes soft after soaking too long paper tears early
Answer

Use the intended wet sanding grit with a fresh piece. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Why it happens
Extended soaking can soften the backing and make handling less controlled.
Recommended grit
Use the intended wet sanding grit with a fresh piece.
Wet or dry
Wet, but only wet the sheet as needed for the sanding step.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
