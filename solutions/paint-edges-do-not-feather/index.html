Problem

Paint edges do not feather smoothly

A paint edge remains visible instead of blending into the surrounding surface.

Answer

Start around 220 or 320, then refine with 400 as needed. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Why it happens
The edge needs gradual leveling and refinement rather than pressure in one spot.
Recommended grit
Start around 220 or 320, then refine with 400 as needed.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on coating stage.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
