Raised grain after water wipe

Wood feels fuzzy or raised after wiping with water.

Solution

Problem
Wood feels fuzzy or raised after wiping with water.
Likely cause
Water can raise wood fibers that need light sanding after the surface dries.
Recommended grit
Use 220 or 320 lightly after the surface dries.
Wet or dry
Dry after the water has dried.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

Steps

  1. Sand with the grain where possible.
  2. Use the grit that removes the visible issue.
  3. Step finer gradually.
  4. Remove dust and inspect under good light.
Avoid: Do not skip grit steps while visible scratches or high spots remain.
