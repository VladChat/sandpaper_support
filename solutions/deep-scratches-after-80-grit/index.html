Problem

Deep scratches remain after 80 grit

80 grit removed material but left deep scratches.

Quick Answer

120, then 180, then 220 or 320 as needed. Clean the surface.

Best Grit Path

120 → 180 → 220 → 320 → 80

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Clean the surface.
  2. Sand evenly with 120 grit until the 80-grit marks are lighter.
  3. Move to 180 grit, then 220 or 320.
  4. Inspect between each grit.
Why It Happens

80 grit is a heavy removal grit and leaves a scratch pattern that must be refined in steps.

Wet or Dry

Dry for early removal and prep.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not jump from 80 directly to 320 or finer.
Success Check

The surface shows one even finer scratch pattern with no remaining 80-grit lines.
