80 grit removes too much

80 grit cuts deeper or faster than expected.

Solution

Problem
80 grit cuts deeper or faster than expected.
Likely cause
80 grit is still a coarse removal grit and can change edges or surface level quickly.
Recommended grit
Move to 120 or 150 for lighter shaping, then 180 and 220.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

Steps

  1. Clean the surface.
  2. Use the recommended grit with light, even pressure.
  3. Inspect before changing grits.
  4. Move finer only after the old marks are gone.
Avoid: Do not skip grit steps while visible scratches or high spots remain.
