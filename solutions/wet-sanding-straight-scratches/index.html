Problem

Wet sanding leaves straight scratches

Straight scratches appear during wet sanding.

Answer

Repeat the current grit after cleaning, or step back one grit only if needed. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Why it happens
A particle, old grit mark, or uneven stroke pattern may be cutting deeper than the current grit.
Recommended grit
Repeat the current grit after cleaning, or step back one grit only if needed.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The old marks are replaced by one uniform finer scratch pattern.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
