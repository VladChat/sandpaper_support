Problem

500 vs 600 grit confusion

It is unclear whether 500 or 600 grit should be used next.

Quick Answer

Use 500 when 400 marks need a smaller step; use 600 for finer prep. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Best Grit Path

500 → 400 → 600

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Why It Happens

Both are fine finishing or coating-prep grits, with 600 leaving a finer pattern.

Wet or Dry

Dry or wet depending on the coating and surface.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
Success Check

The surface looks and feels even before the next step.
