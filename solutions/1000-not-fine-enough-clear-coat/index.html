Problem

1000 grit is not fine enough for clear coat gloss

Clear coat still looks dull after 1000 grit.

Quick Answer

1500, then 2000 and 3000 before polishing. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Best Grit Path

1500 → 2000 → 3000 → 1000

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Why It Happens

1000 grit is commonly a leveling step and usually needs finer wet sanding before polishing.

Wet or Dry

Wet.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not expect sanding alone to create full gloss.
Success Check

The haze is even and ready for the next refinement or polishing step.
