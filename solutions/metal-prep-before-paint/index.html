Metal prep before paint

Metal needs sanding before coating.

Solution

Problem
Metal needs sanding before coating.
Likely cause
The surface needs cleaning and a consistent prep pattern.
Recommended grit
180 to 320 depending on existing roughness and coating instructions.
Wet or dry
Dry for prep; wet only where suitable for finer steps.
Success check
The metal is clean and uniformly prepared.

Steps

  1. Clean the metal.
  2. Remove roughness or oxidation with the needed grit.
  3. Refine to a consistent pattern.
  4. Clean residue before coating.
Avoid: Do not coat over loose debris or deep random scratches.
