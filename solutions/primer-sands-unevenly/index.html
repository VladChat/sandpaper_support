Primer sands unevenly

Primer has patchy dull spots or uneven texture after sanding.

Solution

Problem
Primer has patchy dull spots or uneven texture after sanding.
Likely cause
Pressure, cure state, or dust loading may be uneven across the surface.
Recommended grit
320 to 600 depending on the primer stage and coating instructions.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet where suitable.
Success check
The haze is even and ready for the next refinement or polishing step.

Steps

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
