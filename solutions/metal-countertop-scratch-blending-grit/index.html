Problem

What grit to blend scratches on a metal countertop?

A metal countertop repair can leave a different scratch pattern than the surrounding surface.

wood metal wet sanding dry sanding
Answer

For metal countertop scratches, blend with the grain using 600, then refine with 800 and 1000.

Why it happens
Metal countertops need blending with the existing grain or finish direction.
Recommended grit
Use 600 for light scratches, then 800 and 1000 for finer blending.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on the metal and finish.
Success check
The repaired area blends with the surrounding finish direction.

What to do

  1. Clean the countertop first.
  2. Identify the existing grain direction.
  3. Blend light scratches with 600.
  4. Refine with 800 and 1000.
Avoid: Do not sand against the existing grain.
