Problem

60 grit is too aggressive

60 grit cuts fast and leaves scratches deeper than the project needs.

Answer

Use 80, 120, or 150 instead when heavy removal is not needed. Clean the surface.

Why it happens
60 grit is a heavy removal grit and may be too coarse for light prep or finish work.
Recommended grit
Use 80, 120, or 150 instead when heavy removal is not needed.
Wet or dry
Dry for heavy removal.
Success check
The old marks are replaced by one uniform finer scratch pattern.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface.
  2. Use the recommended grit with light, even pressure.
  3. Inspect before changing grits.
  4. Move finer only after the old marks are gone.
Avoid: Do not skip grit steps while visible scratches or high spots remain.
