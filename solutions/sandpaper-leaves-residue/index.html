Problem

Sandpaper leaves residue

Residue from dust, slurry, or loaded coating remains after sanding.

Quick Answer

Stay with the current grit, then move finer only after cleaning. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Best Grit Path

220 → 320 → 400 → 600

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Why It Happens

Loaded abrasive, dirty wet sanding water, or loose sanding dust is being spread over the surface.

Wet or Dry

Dry or wet depending on the stage.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
Success Check

The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

Related Problems
  • Paint clogs the sheet quickly
  • Primer dust loads the paper
  • Wood dust clogs the paper
