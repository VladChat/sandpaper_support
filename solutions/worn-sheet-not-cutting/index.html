Problem

Worn sheet is not cutting

The sheet feels smooth and sanding no longer changes the surface.

Quick Answer

Stay with the selected grit but use a fresh sheet. Inspect the abrasive surface.

Best Grit Path

120 → 180 → 220 → 320

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Inspect the abrasive surface.
  2. Clear debris if lightly loaded.
  3. Replace the sheet if worn smooth.
  4. Continue with light pressure.
Why It Happens

The abrasive is worn or loaded.

Wet or Dry

Dry or wet depending on the task.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not compensate for a worn sheet with heavy pressure.
Success Check

A fresh sheet cuts noticeably better.

Related Problems

  • Fine grit used for removal
  • Sheet feels smooth but looks unused
