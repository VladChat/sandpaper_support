Problem

What grit removes rust from snowboard edges?

Snowboard edges can develop light rust after storage.

metal dry sanding clogging pressure
Answer

For snowboard edge surface rust, use 400 or 600 lightly without changing the edge shape.

Why it happens
Metal edges need gentle rust removal without changing the edge shape.
Recommended grit
Use 400 or 600 for light surface rust. Use coarser grits only for non-edge body metal repairs.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
Light rust is reduced without changing the edge profile.

What to do

  1. Wipe the edge clean.
  2. Use 400 or 600 lightly on surface rust.
  3. Work along the edge, not across it aggressively.
  4. Clean residue after sanding.
Avoid: Do not reshape or round the metal edge with sandpaper.
