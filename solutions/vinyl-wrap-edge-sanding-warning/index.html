Problem

Should I sand vinyl wrap edges?

Vinyl wrap edges can tear, lift, or look ragged if sanded.

paint scratches
Answer

Do not sand vinyl wrap edges. Remove or trim damaged wrap, then prep the underlying surface if needed.

Why it happens
Vinyl wrap is a thin film, not a surface meant for abrasive sanding.
Recommended grit
Do not sand vinyl wrap edges. Remove or trim damaged wrap instead of sanding it.
Wet or dry
No sanding recommended.
Success check
The wrap is not torn or scratched by abrasive sanding.

What to do

  1. Clean the wrap edge.
  2. Do not sand the vinyl film.
  3. Trim or replace damaged wrap where needed.
  4. Prep the underlying surface only after the wrap is removed.
Avoid: Do not use sandpaper directly on vinyl wrap.
