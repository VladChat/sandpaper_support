Problem

What grit for pallet wood?

Pallet wood can be rough, splintery, and uneven before finishing.

wood metal paint dry sanding
Answer

For pallet wood, start with 80 or 100, then use 120, 180, and 220.

Why it happens
Reclaimed wood often has saw marks, dirt, and raised fibers that need step-by-step smoothing.
Recommended grit
Use 80 or 100 for rough cleanup, then 120, 180, and 220 for smoother prep.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The wood feels smooth with no loose splinters before finishing.

What to do

  1. Remove dirt, fasteners, and loose splinters first.
  2. Start with 80 or 100 on rough areas.
  3. Move through 120 and 180.
  4. Finish with 220 before paint, stain, or clear finish.
Avoid: Do not start with fine grit on splintery pallet wood.
