Problem

Paint removal is too slow

Paint is not coming off efficiently.

paint / primer material removal 60 → 80 → 120 paint removal is too slow paint is not coming off efficiently. paint / primer paint is not coming off efficiently how to fix paint removal is too slow sanding takes too long
Answer

60, 80, or 120 for removal depending on the surface and finish goal. Use a coarser grit suited to removal.

Why it happens
The grit may be too fine, loaded, or worn for removal.
Recommended grit
60, 80, or 120 for removal depending on the surface and finish goal.
Wet or dry
Dry for removal.
Success check
Paint removal speeds up without excessive pressure.

What to do

  1. Use a coarser grit suited to removal.
  2. Clear dust and paint residue often.
  3. Replace worn paper.
  4. Refine scratches with finer grits afterward.
Avoid: Do not remove paint with 400+ grit unless only light scuffing is needed.
