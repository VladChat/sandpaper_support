Problem

Veneer sands through

A thin wood surface is sanded through to a lower layer.

wood scratch removal 180 → 320 veneer sands through a thin wood surface is sanded through to a lower layer. wood thin wood surface is sanded through to a lower layer how to fix veneer sands through scratches too deep
Answer

Use fine prep grits such as 180 to 320 with very light pressure. Sand with the grain where possible.

Why it happens
Coarse grit or heavy pressure removed too much material from a thin surface.
Recommended grit
Use fine prep grits such as 180 to 320 with very light pressure.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Sand with the grain where possible.
  2. Use the grit that removes the visible issue.
  3. Step finer gradually.
  4. Remove dust and inspect under good light.
Avoid: Do not skip grit steps while visible scratches or high spots remain.
