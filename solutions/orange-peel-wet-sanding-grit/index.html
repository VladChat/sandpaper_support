Problem

What grit should I use for orange peel?

The user wants to reduce orange peel texture in a suitable finish.

clear coat wet sanding between coats scratches
Answer

Use the finest grit that levels the orange peel, commonly 1000 or 1500, then refine with 2000 and 3000.

Why it happens
Orange peel is surface texture that may need controlled leveling before fine refinement.
Recommended grit
Use the finest grit that levels the texture, commonly 1000 or 1500, then refine with 2000 and 3000.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
Texture is reduced evenly without deep scratches or thin spots.

What to do

  1. Confirm the coating is ready for sanding.
  2. Start with the finest grit that levels the texture.
  3. Use a flat backing where appropriate.
  4. Refine through 2000 and 3000.
Avoid: Do not sand through edges or high spots.
