Problem

Can this sandpaper be used wet and dry?

The user wants to know whether the sandpaper can be used wet or dry.

wood wet sanding dry sanding between coats
Answer

Yes, these silicon carbide sheets are for wet or dry use. Use dry for rough prep and wet for suitable fine finishing stages.

Why it happens
Wet or dry choice depends on the surface, grit, and finish stage.
Recommended grit
Use coarse and medium grits dry for shaping; use fine and ultra-fine grits wet for suitable finishing work.
Wet or dry
Wet or dry use.
Success check
The sanding method matches the surface and the finish stage.

What to do

  1. Choose dry sanding for rough removal or wood prep.
  2. Choose wet sanding for suitable fine finishing stages.
  3. Use clean water when wet sanding.
  4. Let the surface dry before judging the final result.
Avoid: Do not wet sand surfaces or coatings that should stay dry.
