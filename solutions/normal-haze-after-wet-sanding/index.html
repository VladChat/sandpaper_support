Problem

Normal haze after wet sanding

Wet sanding leaves the surface dull or cloudy.

automotive / clear coat wet sanding refinement 1000 → 1500 → 2000 → 3000 normal haze after wet sanding wet sanding leaves the surface dull or cloudy. automotive / clear coat wet sanding leaves the surface dull or cloudy how to fix normal haze after wet sanding wet sanding leaves haze
Answer

1000, 1500, 2000, then 3000 as needed. Keep the surface wet.

Why it happens
Fine wet sanding normally creates a uniform haze before polishing or final refinement.
Recommended grit
1000, 1500, 2000, then 3000 as needed.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The haze is even and free of deeper scratches.

What to do

  1. Keep the surface wet.
  2. Use light pressure.
  3. Move through finer grits.
  4. Polish after the final grit when gloss is required.
Avoid: Do not expect sanding alone to restore gloss.
