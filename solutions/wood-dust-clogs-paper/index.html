Wood dust clogs the paper

Wood dust fills the abrasive and the sheet feels smooth.

Solution

Problem
Wood dust fills the abrasive and the sheet feels smooth.
Likely cause
Dust buildup, excess pressure, or a worn sheet is reducing cutting.
Recommended grit
Use the correct prep grit, commonly 120 to 220 for wood smoothing.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
Fresh abrasive texture remains visible and the surface smooths evenly.

Steps

  1. Brush or wipe dust away often.
  2. Use lighter pressure.
  3. Use a sanding block on flat areas.
  4. Replace worn sheets.
Avoid: Do not use a clogged sheet for final prep.
