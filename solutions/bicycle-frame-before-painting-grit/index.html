Problem

What grit for bicycle frame before painting?

Paint on a bicycle frame can peel or show scratches when prep is uneven.

metal paint primer wet sanding
Answer

For a bicycle frame, scuff old paint with 220 or 320, then refine with 400 or 600.

Why it happens
Old paint, bare metal, and primer areas need a clean scuffed surface before repainting.
Recommended grit
Use 220 or 320 for old paint scuffing, then 400 or 600 before primer or topcoat.
Wet or dry
Dry for shaping; wet for finer finishing stages.
Success check
The frame is clean, dull, and evenly prepared for coating.

What to do

  1. Clean the frame thoroughly.
  2. Scuff old paint with 220 or 320.
  3. Use 400 or 600 for finer paint prep.
  4. Remove all dust before primer or paint.
Avoid: Do not leave glossy old paint under new paint.
