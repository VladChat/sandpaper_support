Problem

150 grit is not smooth enough

The surface still feels rough after sanding with 150 grit.

Answer

Move to 180, then 220 if a smoother prep surface is needed. Clean the surface.

Why it happens
150 grit is a prep step and usually needs follow-up refinement for smoother surfaces.
Recommended grit
Move to 180, then 220 if a smoother prep surface is needed.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface.
  2. Use the recommended grit with light, even pressure.
  3. Inspect before changing grits.
  4. Move finer only after the old marks are gone.
Avoid: Do not skip grit steps while visible scratches or high spots remain.

Ask a follow-up

Ask about this answer, the next grit, or wet/dry use.
