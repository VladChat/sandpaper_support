Problem

What grit removes double-sided tape residue?

Double-sided tape residue remains sticky or raised after peeling.

paint wet sanding dry sanding clogging
Answer

Remove bulk tape residue first, then sand lightly with 220 or 320. Use 400 for final prep.

Why it happens
Adhesive residue can smear under pressure and clog sandpaper quickly.
Recommended grit
Remove bulk adhesive first, then use 220 or 320 lightly. Use 400 for final prep.
Wet or dry
Dry for most surfaces; wet only where the surface allows it.
Success check
The surface is smooth, clean, and no longer sticky.

What to do

  1. Remove as much adhesive as possible before sanding.
  2. Use light pressure with 220 or 320.
  3. Wipe residue often.
  4. Finish with 400 before painting or refinishing.
Avoid: Do not grind sticky adhesive with heavy pressure.
