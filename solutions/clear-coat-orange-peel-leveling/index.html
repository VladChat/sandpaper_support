Problem

Clear coat leveling before polish

Clear coat needs fine leveling before polishing.

automotive / clear coat wet sanding refinement 1000 → 1500 → 2000 → 3000 clear coat leveling before polish clear coat needs fine leveling before polishing. automotive / clear coat clear coat needs fine leveling before polishing how to fix clear coat leveling before polish wet sanding leaves haze
Answer

1000 or 1500 to start only when appropriate, then 2000 and 3000. Confirm the coating is ready for sanding.

Why it happens
The surface has texture that requires controlled fine wet sanding.
Recommended grit
1000 or 1500 to start only when appropriate, then 2000 and 3000.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
Texture is leveled and the haze is uniform before polishing.

What to do

  1. Confirm the coating is ready for sanding.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Keep the area evenly sanded.
  4. Progress finer before polishing.
Avoid: Do not sand through edges or raised body lines.
