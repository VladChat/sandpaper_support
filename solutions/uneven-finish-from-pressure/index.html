Problem

Uneven finish from pressure

The sanded surface looks patchy or uneven.

drywall patch wet sanding dry sanding scratches
Answer

Repeat the current stage grit before moving finer. Sand with overlapping strokes.

Why it happens
Pressure or stroke coverage was inconsistent.
Recommended grit
Repeat the current stage grit before moving finer.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on the stage.
Success check
The scratch pattern and sheen are uniform.

What to do

  1. Sand with overlapping strokes.
  2. Use even pressure.
  3. Use a block on flat surfaces.
  4. Clean and inspect before changing grits.
Avoid: Do not focus pressure in one small area.
