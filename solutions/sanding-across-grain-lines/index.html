Problem

Sanding across grain leaves lines

Lines across the wood grain remain visible after sanding.

Answer

Return to 150 or 180 if needed, then finish with 220 along the grain. Sand with the grain where possible.

Why it happens
Cross-grain scratches are more visible and need careful refinement.
Recommended grit
Return to 150 or 180 if needed, then finish with 220 along the grain.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Sand with the grain where possible.
  2. Use the grit that removes the visible issue.
  3. Step finer gradually.
  4. Remove dust and inspect under good light.
Avoid: Do not skip grit steps while visible scratches or high spots remain.

Ask a follow-up

Ask about this answer, the next grit, or wet/dry use.
