Problem

What is 400 grit sandpaper used for?

The user wants to know when 400 grit is appropriate.

primer wet sanding dry sanding between coats
Answer

400 grit is for fine prep and refinement after 320 and before 600.

Why it happens
400 grit is a fine grit for surface refinement, primer prep, and light smoothing.
Recommended grit
Use 400 after 320 or before 600 when a finer prep is needed.
Wet or dry
Dry for fine prep; wet for fine finishing stages.
Success check
The surface is refined without deep scratches.

What to do

  1. Use 400 after the surface is already level.
  2. Sand lightly.
  3. Clean before moving to 600 or finishing.
  4. Check coating instructions for final prep requirements.
Avoid: Do not use 400 for heavy removal.
