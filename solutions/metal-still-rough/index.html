Problem

Metal still feels rough

Metal still has roughness after sanding.

metal surface refinement 120 → 180 → 220 → 320 metal still feels rough metal still has roughness after sanding. metal metal still has roughness after sanding how to fix metal still feels rough surface still feels rough
Answer

Start around 120 or 180 for roughness, then 220 and 320. Remove loose debris.

Why it happens
Burrs, oxidation, or deeper scratches need a grit that still cuts them.
Recommended grit
Start around 120 or 180 for roughness, then 220 and 320.
Wet or dry
Dry for initial prep; wet for finer stages where suitable.
Success check
The metal feels even and the scratch pattern is consistent.

What to do

  1. Remove loose debris.
  2. Sand with a grit that cuts the roughness.
  3. Step finer gradually.
  4. Clean before final inspection.
Avoid: Do not start too fine on rough metal.
