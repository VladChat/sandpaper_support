Finger grooves from hand sanding

The surface has uneven grooves from fingers.

Solution

Problem
The surface has uneven grooves from fingers.
Likely cause
Finger pressure concentrated force in narrow lines.
Recommended grit
Use the current prep grit, then refine normally.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on stage.
Success check
The surface no longer shows narrow pressure tracks.

Steps

  1. Use a sanding block on flat surfaces.
  2. Keep pressure even.
  3. Blend the affected area.
  4. Move finer only when the surface is even.
Avoid: Do not press with fingertips on flat finish areas.
