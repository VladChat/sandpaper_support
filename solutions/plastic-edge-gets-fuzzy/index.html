Problem

Plastic edge gets fuzzy

A plastic edge becomes fuzzy or rough after sanding.

plastic surface refinement 400 → 600 plastic edge gets fuzzy a plastic edge becomes fuzzy or rough after sanding. plastic plastic edge becomes fuzzy or rough after sanding how to fix plastic edge gets fuzzy surface still feels rough
Answer

Use 400 to 600, then finer if needed. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Why it happens
Pressure or coarse grit can raise a rough edge instead of refining it cleanly.
Recommended grit
Use 400 to 600, then finer if needed.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on the plastic and stage.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
