Clear coat cloudy after 2000 grit

Clear coat remains cloudy after 2000 grit wet sanding.

Solution

Problem
Clear coat remains cloudy after 2000 grit wet sanding.
Likely cause
2000 grit still leaves a fine haze and may need 3000 and polishing for gloss.
Recommended grit
Refine with 3000, then polish if gloss is required.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

Steps

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not expect sanding alone to create full gloss.
