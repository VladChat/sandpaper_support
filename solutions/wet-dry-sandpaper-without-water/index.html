Problem

Can wet/dry sandpaper be used without water?

Wet/dry sandpaper can confuse customers because the name includes wet and dry.

wood plastic clear coat wet sanding
Answer

Yes. Wet/dry sandpaper can be used dry. Use wet sanding mainly for fine finishing and polishing prep.

Why it happens
Wet/dry sandpaper is designed for both methods, but the better method depends on the surface and finish stage.
Recommended grit
Use dry sanding for early shaping and wood prep. Use wet sanding for fine finishing, clear coat, plastic, resin, and polishing prep.
Wet or dry
Wet or dry.
Success check
The sanding method matches the surface and the final finish goal.

What to do

  1. Use dry sanding for removal, shaping, and bare wood prep.
  2. Use wet sanding for fine finishing stages.
  3. Keep wet sanding clean with fresh water.
  4. Match the method to the surface and finish goal.
Avoid: Do not wet sand bare wood as a default method.
