Problem

Primer dust loads the paper

Primer dust packs into the abrasive during prep.

Quick Answer

320 to 600 depending on the coating stage. Let the primer cure as directed by the coating maker.

Best Grit Path

320 → 600

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Let the primer cure as directed by the coating maker.
  2. Use light pressure.
  3. Clear dust frequently.
  4. Switch sheets when cutting slows.
Why It Happens

Dust is not being cleared often enough or the sheet is worn.

Wet or Dry

Dry or wet depending on coating instructions and finish stage.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not sand uncured coating.
Success Check

The surface powders cleanly and the sheet keeps cutting.

Related Problems
  • Paint clogs the sheet quickly
  • Wood dust clogs the paper
  • Finish smears while sanding
