Problem

Using the assorted kit for polishing prep

A glossy surface needs fine sanding before polish.

automotive / clear coat wet sanding refinement 1000 → 1500 → 2000 → 3000 using the assorted kit for polishing prep a glossy surface needs fine sanding before polish. automotive / clear coat glossy surface needs fine sanding before polish how to fix using the assorted kit for polishing prep wet sanding leaves haze
Answer

1000, 1500, 2000, and 3000. Start only as coarse as the defect requires.

Why it happens
The surface must be refined through high grits before polishing.
Recommended grit
1000, 1500, 2000, and 3000.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The final haze is very fine and uniform.

What to do

  1. Start only as coarse as the defect requires.
  2. Keep the surface wet.
  3. Use light pressure.
  4. Polish after the final fine grit if gloss is required.
Avoid: Do not use low grits for polishing prep.
