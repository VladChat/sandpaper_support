Problem

Using the assorted kit for polishing prep

A glossy surface needs fine sanding before polish.

Quick Answer

1000, 1500, 2000, and 3000. Start only as coarse as the defect requires.

Best Grit Path

1000 → 1500 → 2000 → 3000

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Start only as coarse as the defect requires.
  2. Keep the surface wet.
  3. Use light pressure.
  4. Polish after the final fine grit if gloss is required.
Why It Happens

The surface must be refined through high grits before polishing.

Wet or Dry

Wet.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not use low grits for polishing prep.
Success Check

The final haze is very fine and uniform.

Related Problems
  • Normal haze after wet sanding
  • Clear coat haze after 1000 grit
  • Plastic haze after wet sanding
