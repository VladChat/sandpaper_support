Patch edge visible after sanding

The edge of a drywall patch still shows after sanding.

Solution

Problem
The edge of a drywall patch still shows after sanding.
Likely cause
The patch edge was not feathered gradually into the surrounding wall.
Recommended grit
Use 180, then 220 for final feathering.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

Steps

  1. Let the patch dry fully.
  2. Sand the high area with light pressure.
  3. Feather the edge into the surrounding surface.
  4. Remove dust before painting.
Avoid: Do not press hard enough to tear paper or create low spots.
