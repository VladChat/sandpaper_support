Problem

Patch is smooth but shows after paint

The patch feels smooth but is visible after painting.

drywall surface refinement 180 → 220 patch is smooth but shows after paint the patch feels smooth but is visible after painting. drywall patch feels smooth but is visible after painting how to fix patch is smooth but shows after paint finish looks uneven
Answer

Use 180 to feather, then 220 for light final smoothing. Let the patch dry fully.

Why it happens
Uneven scratch pattern, dust, or an unfeathered edge can show once paint is applied.
Recommended grit
Use 180 to feather, then 220 for light final smoothing.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Let the patch dry fully.
  2. Sand the high area with light pressure.
  3. Feather the edge into the surrounding surface.
  4. Remove dust before painting.
Avoid: Do not press hard enough to tear paper or create low spots.
