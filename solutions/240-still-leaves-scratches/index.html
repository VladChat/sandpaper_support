Problem

240 grit still leaves scratches

Scratches remain visible after 240 grit.

Answer

Use 180 or 220 first if needed, then 240 and 320. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Why it happens
The previous grit marks were too deep or 240 is still not the final finish grit for the surface.
Recommended grit
Use 180 or 220 first if needed, then 240 and 320.
Wet or dry
Dry for prep; wet only for suitable fine stages.
Success check
The old marks are replaced by one uniform finer scratch pattern.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
