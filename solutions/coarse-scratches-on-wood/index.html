Coarse scratches show on wood

Wood still shows visible scratch lines after prep sanding.

Solution

Problem
Wood still shows visible scratch lines after prep sanding.
Likely cause
The previous grit pattern was not fully removed before moving finer.
Recommended grit
120 to remove heavy marks, then 180 and 220 for prep.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The wood feels even and has no isolated deep scratch lines.

Steps

  1. Sand with the grain when possible.
  2. Use the grit that still removes the visible lines.
  3. Step through 180 and 220.
  4. Remove dust and inspect under good light.
Avoid: Do not skip directly to fine finishing grits on rough wood.
