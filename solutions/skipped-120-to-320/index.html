Problem

Skipped from 120 to 320

320 grit does not remove the scratches left by 120 grit.

Answer

Use 180 or 220 before 320. Return to 180 or 220.

Why it happens
The grit progression skipped too many intermediate steps.
Recommended grit
Use 180 or 220 before 320.
Wet or dry
Dry for prep.
Success check
Each grit removes the previous grit pattern before moving on.

What to do

  1. Return to 180 or 220.
  2. Sand until the 120-grit lines are gone.
  3. Clean the surface.
  4. Move to 320.
Avoid: Do not use fine grit as a substitute for proper progression.

Ask a follow-up

Ask about this answer, the next grit, or wet/dry use.
