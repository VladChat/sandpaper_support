Problem

Sheet curls during wet sanding

The sheet curls up and becomes hard to keep flat while wet sanding.

Quick Answer

Use the planned wet sanding grit, commonly 800 to 3000 for fine stages. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Best Grit Path

800 → 3000

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Why It Happens

Water exposure and hand pressure can make a cut piece curl, especially if it is not supported evenly.

Wet or Dry

Wet.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not expect sanding alone to create full gloss.
Success Check

The surface looks and feels even before the next step.
