Problem

Wet sanding creates random deep scratches

Random deeper scratches appear during wet sanding.

Quick Answer

Clean and repeat the current grit, then refine finer. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Best Grit Path

120 → 180 → 220 → 320

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Why It Happens

Loose abrasive, dirt, or contaminated slurry can drag under the sheet.

Wet or Dry

Wet.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
Success Check

The old marks are replaced by one uniform finer scratch pattern.
