Wet sanding haze is uneven

Some areas are dull while others are still glossy or scratched.

Solution

Problem
Some areas are dull while others are still glossy or scratched.
Likely cause
Pressure, water, or sanding coverage was inconsistent.
Recommended grit
Repeat the current fine grit before moving finer.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The entire area has a consistent fine haze.

Steps

  1. Rinse the surface.
  2. Sand with overlapping strokes.
  3. Keep water consistent.
  4. Move finer only when the haze is even.
Avoid: Do not polish an unevenly sanded surface and expect uniform results.
