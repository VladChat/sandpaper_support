Problem

Wet sanding haze is uneven

Some areas are dull while others are still glossy or scratched.

automotive / clear coat wet sanding refinement wet sanding haze is uneven some areas are dull while others are still glossy or scratched. automotive / clear coat some areas are dull while others are still glossy or scratched how to fix wet sanding haze is uneven wet sanding leaves haze
Answer

Repeat the current fine grit before moving finer. Rinse the surface.

Why it happens
Pressure, water, or sanding coverage was inconsistent.
Recommended grit
Repeat the current fine grit before moving finer.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The entire area has a consistent fine haze.

What to do

  1. Rinse the surface.
  2. Sand with overlapping strokes.
  3. Keep water consistent.
  4. Move finer only when the haze is even.
Avoid: Do not polish an unevenly sanded surface and expect uniform results.
