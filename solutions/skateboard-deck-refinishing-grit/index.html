Problem

What grit for refinishing a skateboard deck?

A skateboard deck can have old finish, scratches, or rough wood after grip tape removal.

wood paint dry sanding scratches
Answer

For a skateboard deck, remove adhesive first, then sand with 120 or 180, 220, and 320.

Why it happens
Adhesive residue and old finish need cleanup before smooth refinishing.
Recommended grit
Use 120 or 180 for cleanup, then 220 and 320 for finish prep.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The deck surface is smooth, clean, and ready for finish.

What to do

  1. Remove grip tape residue first.
  2. Sand rough areas with 120 or 180.
  3. Refine with 220.
  4. Use 320 before clear finish or paint.
Avoid: Do not sand through thin deck layers.
