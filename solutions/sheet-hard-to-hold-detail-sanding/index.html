Problem

Sheet is hard to hold for detail sanding

Small details are difficult to sand evenly with a loose sheet.

wet sanding dry sanding pressure medium grit
Answer

Use the grit required for the detail stage, often 180 to 400 for prep. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Why it happens
A large piece of sheet can bunch up and concentrate pressure in small areas.
Recommended grit
Use the grit required for the detail stage, often 180 to 400 for prep.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on the surface and stage.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
