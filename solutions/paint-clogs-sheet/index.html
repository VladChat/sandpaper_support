Problem

Paint clogs the sheet quickly

Paint residue loads into the abrasive and sanding slows.

Quick Answer

Use the grit needed for the stage; often 120 to 220 for removal or prep. Clean the painted surface.

Best Grit Path

120 → 220

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Clean the painted surface.
  2. Use lighter pressure.
  3. Wipe the sheet often.
  4. Replace loaded sheets promptly.
Why It Happens

Soft paint or coating residue is smearing under pressure.

Wet or Dry

Dry for removal; wet only for suitable fine finishing stages.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not keep sanding with a glazed or loaded sheet.
Success Check

The paper cuts instead of smearing paint residue.

Related Problems
  • Primer dust loads the paper
  • Wood dust clogs the paper
  • Finish smears while sanding
