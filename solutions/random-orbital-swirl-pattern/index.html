Problem

Random swirl pattern remains

A visible pattern remains after sanding.

general sandpaper use scratch removal random swirl pattern remains a visible pattern remains after sanding. general sandpaper use visible pattern remains after sanding how to fix random swirl pattern remains swirl marks remain
Answer

Move gradually through finer grits up to the finish goal. Identify the deepest visible marks.

Why it happens
The final grit was not fine enough or the previous scratches were not fully removed.
Recommended grit
Move gradually through finer grits up to the finish goal.
Wet or dry
Wet for fine finish stages.
Success check
The pattern is fine, uniform, and ready for polishing or finishing.

What to do

  1. Identify the deepest visible marks.
  2. Return one grit step if needed.
  3. Sand uniformly.
  4. Progress finer only after the pattern is even.
Avoid: Do not skip refinement steps when the finish will be inspected closely.
