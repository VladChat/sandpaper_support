Paint looks dull after sanding

Paint or clear coat looks dull after fine sanding.

Solution

Problem
Paint or clear coat looks dull after fine sanding.
Likely cause
Sanding creates a uniform scratch pattern or haze before the next coating or polish step.
Recommended grit
Use the grit required by the stage, often 600+ for fine prep or 1000-3000 for polishing prep.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on coating and stage.
Success check
The haze is even and ready for the next refinement or polishing step.

Steps

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not expect sanding alone to create full gloss.
