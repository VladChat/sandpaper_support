Deep lines remain on clear coat

Fine sanding leaves some deeper lines in clear coat.

Solution

Problem
Fine sanding leaves some deeper lines in clear coat.
Likely cause
A previous grit or debris created scratches deeper than the current fine grit can remove.
Recommended grit
Return one step coarser only as needed, then progress 1000, 1500, 2000, 3000.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The haze is uniform with no isolated deep lines.

Steps

  1. Rinse the surface and paper.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine only the affected area evenly.
  4. Move finer once the deeper lines are gone.
Avoid: Do not dry sand a fine clear coat finish unless the coating instructions call for it.
