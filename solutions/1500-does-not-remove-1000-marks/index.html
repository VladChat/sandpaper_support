Problem

1500 grit does not remove 1000 marks

1000-grit marks remain after sanding with 1500.

wet sanding scratches pressure ultra fine grit
Answer

Repeat 1500 with light pressure, or return to 1000 only where deeper marks remain. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Why it happens
The 1000-grit scratches were not uniform or the 1500 step has not fully refined them yet.
Recommended grit
Repeat 1500 with light pressure, or return to 1000 only where deeper marks remain.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The old marks are replaced by one uniform finer scratch pattern.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
