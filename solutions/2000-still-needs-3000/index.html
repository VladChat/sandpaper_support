Problem

2000 grit still needs 3000

The surface is finer after 2000 grit but still not ready for the desired gloss.

automotive / clear coat wet sanding refinement 2000 → 3000 2000 grit still needs 3000 the surface is finer after 2000 grit but still not ready for the desired gloss. automotive / clear coat surface is finer after 2000 grit but still not ready for the desired gloss how to fix 2000 grit still needs 3000 wet sanding leaves haze
Answer

Move to 3000 before polishing when a finer finish is needed. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Why it happens
2000 grit can leave a fine haze that may still need 3000 and polishing for gloss.
Recommended grit
Move to 3000 before polishing when a finer finish is needed.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not expect sanding alone to create full gloss.
