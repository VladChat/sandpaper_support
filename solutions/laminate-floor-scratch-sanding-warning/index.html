Problem

Can I sand laminate floor scratches?

Laminate flooring can lose its printed wear layer if sanded.

wood dry sanding scratches pressure
Answer

Do not sand laminate flooring like hardwood. For tiny repair prep only, test 600 or finer in a hidden spot.

Why it happens
Laminate floors have a thin decorative surface that cannot be sanded like hardwood.
Recommended grit
Avoid sanding laminate floors. For tiny repair prep only, test 600 or finer in a hidden spot.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The test area remains unchanged except for the intended repair prep.

What to do

  1. Clean the floor first.
  2. Do not sand laminate like real hardwood.
  3. For repair prep, test 600 or finer in a hidden area.
  4. Stop if the top layer changes color or sheen.
Avoid: Do not use coarse grit on laminate flooring.
