Problem

Swirls from dirty wet sanding water

Fine sanding creates random curved scratches.

general sandpaper use scratch removal swirls from dirty wet sanding water fine sanding creates random curved scratches. general sandpaper use fine sanding creates random curved scratches how to fix swirls from dirty wet sanding water swirl marks remain
Answer

Repeat the current fine grit, then move finer. Rinse the surface.

Why it happens
Loose grit or debris in the water is scratching the surface.
Recommended grit
Repeat the current fine grit, then move finer.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
New scratches stop appearing and the haze is even.

What to do

  1. Rinse the surface.
  2. Use clean water.
  3. Rinse the paper often.
  4. Wipe before moving to the next grit.
Avoid: Do not keep sanding with contaminated slurry.
