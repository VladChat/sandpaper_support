Problem

Plastic dust clogs paper

Plastic dust packs into the abrasive and slows sanding.

Quick Answer

Use 320 to 600 for prep and replace loaded sheets promptly. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Best Grit Path

320 → 600

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Why It Happens

Plastic residue can load the sheet when pressure or heat is too high.

Wet or Dry

Dry or wet depending on the stage.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
Success Check

The abrasive cuts cleanly without packed residue.

Related Problems
  • Paint clogs the sheet quickly
  • Primer dust loads the paper
  • Wood dust clogs the paper
