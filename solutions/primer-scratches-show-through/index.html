Problem

Primer scratches show through

Scratches remain visible after coating over primer.

Quick Answer

320 or 400 before recoating, depending on coating instructions. Sand the primer evenly.

Best Grit Path

320 → 400

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Sand the primer evenly.
  2. Remove coarse scratch marks.
  3. Clean all residue.
  4. Recoat only after the surface is uniform.
Why It Happens

The primer was not refined enough before the next coat.

Wet or Dry

Dry or wet as suitable for the primer stage.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not coat over isolated deep sanding marks.
Success Check

The primer has a uniform dull surface before coating.

Related Problems

  • Sanding cuts through between coats
  • Paint edges do not feather smoothly
