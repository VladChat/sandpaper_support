Problem

Primer scratches show through

Scratches remain visible after coating over primer.

paint / primer between-coats prep 320 → 400 primer scratches show through scratches remain visible after coating over primer. paint / primer scratches remain visible after coating over primer how to fix primer scratches show through poor results between coats
Answer

320 or 400 before recoating, depending on coating instructions. Sand the primer evenly.

Why it happens
The primer was not refined enough before the next coat.
Recommended grit
320 or 400 before recoating, depending on coating instructions.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet as suitable for the primer stage.
Success check
The primer has a uniform dull surface before coating.

What to do

  1. Sand the primer evenly.
  2. Remove coarse scratch marks.
  3. Clean all residue.
  4. Recoat only after the surface is uniform.
Avoid: Do not coat over isolated deep sanding marks.
