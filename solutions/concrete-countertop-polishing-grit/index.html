Problem

What grit for concrete countertop polishing?

Concrete countertops can stay dull or show sanding marks after prep.

wet sanding dry sanding polishing prep haze
Answer

For concrete countertops, wet sand through 400, 600, 800, 1000, then 1500 or 2000 before sealing or polishing.

Why it happens
Concrete needs a gradual grit progression before sealing or polishing steps.
Recommended grit
Use 400, 600, 800, 1000, then 1500 or 2000 for a smoother finish.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The surface has a consistent fine finish with no obvious scratch lines.

What to do

  1. Clean loose dust and debris.
  2. Wet sand with 400 or 600 to refine the surface.
  3. Progress through 800, 1000, then 1500 or 2000.
  4. Clean slurry before sealing or polishing.
Avoid: Do not skip grits when trying to remove visible scratches.
