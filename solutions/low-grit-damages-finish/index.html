Problem

Low grit damages finish

A coarse grit leaves heavy scratches or cuts through a finish.

paint / primer scratch removal 320 → 600 low grit damages finish a coarse grit leaves heavy scratches or cuts through a finish. paint / primer coarse grit leaves heavy scratches or cuts through a finish how to fix low grit damages finish scratches too deep
Answer

Use 320 to 600 for light finish prep, depending on the coating stage. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Why it happens
The grit is too aggressive for finish prep or intercoat sanding.
Recommended grit
Use 320 to 600 for light finish prep, depending on the coating stage.
Wet or dry
Light dry sanding or wet sanding where suitable.
Success check
The old marks are replaced by one uniform finer scratch pattern.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
