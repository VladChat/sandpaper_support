Problem

Grit comes off the sheet

Loose abrasive appears on the surface or the sheet sheds grit during sanding.

Answer

Use a fresh sheet in the same grit, or step coarser only if the task is too slow. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Why it happens
The sheet may be worn, creased, over-soaked, or used with heavy pressure on a rough edge.
Recommended grit
Use a fresh sheet in the same grit, or step coarser only if the task is too slow.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on the task; avoid soaking longer than needed.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
