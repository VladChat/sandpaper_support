Problem

Using the assorted kit for finish prep

The surface needs fine prep before coating or finishing.

Answer

220, 320, 400, or 600 depending on finish stage. Use 220 or 320 to refine prep scratches.

Why it happens
Finish prep needs finer grits than heavy removal.
Recommended grit
220, 320, 400, or 600 depending on finish stage.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on coating and stage.
Success check
The surface is smooth, clean, and evenly prepared.

What to do

  1. Use 220 or 320 to refine prep scratches.
  2. Use 400 or 600 for lighter fine prep where appropriate.
  3. Clean fully before coating.
  4. Check that no coarse scratches remain.
Avoid: Do not use 80 or 120 for fine finish prep.
