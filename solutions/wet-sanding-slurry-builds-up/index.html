Problem

Wet sanding slurry builds up

Wet sanding residue collects and makes cutting inconsistent.

general sandpaper use clog control 1000 → 3000 wet sanding slurry builds up wet sanding residue collects and makes cutting inconsistent. general sandpaper use wet sanding residue collects and makes cutting inconsistent how to fix wet sanding slurry builds up paper clogs too fast
Answer

Use the planned fine grit sequence, such as 1000 to 3000. Keep clean water on the surface.

Why it happens
The surface and sheet are not being rinsed often enough.
Recommended grit
Use the planned fine grit sequence, such as 1000 to 3000.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The wet sanding haze stays even and controlled.

What to do

  1. Keep clean water on the surface.
  2. Rinse the sheet often.
  3. Wipe the surface between grits.
  4. Continue with light pressure.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry dry on the work area.
