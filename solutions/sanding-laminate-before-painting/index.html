Problem

What grit for laminate before painting?

Paint can peel from laminate when the glossy surface is not scuffed.

drywall patch paint primer dry sanding
Answer

For laminate before painting, clean first, scuff with 220, then refine lightly with 320.

Why it happens
Laminate is slick and non-porous, so paint needs a clean, dull surface to grip.
Recommended grit
Use 220 to scuff laminate, then 320 for a finer primer-ready surface.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The laminate looks evenly dull with no shiny patches before primer.

What to do

  1. Clean grease and residue from the laminate.
  2. Scuff the surface evenly with 220 grit.
  3. Refine lightly with 320 grit.
  4. Remove all dust before primer or paint.
Avoid: Do not paint over glossy laminate without sanding.
