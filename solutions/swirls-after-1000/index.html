Problem

Swirls remain after 1000 grit

Curved or circular marks remain after fine sanding.

Answer

1500, 2000, then 3000 as needed. Clean the area.

Why it happens
1000 grit marks may need further refinement or the pattern was inconsistent.
Recommended grit
1500, 2000, then 3000 as needed.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
Circular marks are replaced by a uniform fine haze.

What to do

  1. Clean the area.
  2. Use light pressure.
  3. Refine with 1500 grit.
  4. Continue finer until marks are uniform.
Avoid: Do not press harder with the same grit to chase swirls.
