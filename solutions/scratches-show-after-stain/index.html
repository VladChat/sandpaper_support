Scratches show after stain

Scratch lines become visible after stain is applied.

Solution

Problem
Scratch lines become visible after stain is applied.
Likely cause
Dust or lighting hid coarse scratches before finishing.
Recommended grit
Return to 180 or 220 for wood prep, then clean thoroughly.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The old marks are replaced by one uniform finer scratch pattern.

Steps

  1. Sand with the grain where possible.
  2. Use the grit that removes the visible issue.
  3. Step finer gradually.
  4. Remove dust and inspect under good light.
Avoid: Do not skip grit steps while visible scratches or high spots remain.
