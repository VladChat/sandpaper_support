Problem

3000 grit does not create gloss by itself

The surface is still hazy after 3000 grit.

automotive / clear coat wet sanding refinement 3000 3000 grit does not create gloss by itself the surface is still hazy after 3000 grit. automotive / clear coat surface is still hazy after 3000 grit how to fix 3000 grit does not create gloss by itself wet sanding leaves haze
Answer

Use 3000 as the final sanding step before polishing when gloss is required. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Why it happens
3000 grit prepares a very fine sanding haze, but sanding alone does not create full gloss.
Recommended grit
Use 3000 as the final sanding step before polishing when gloss is required.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not expect sanding alone to create full gloss.
