Problem

Clear coat burns through on edges

Clear coat is sanded through on corners, edges, or raised lines.

Quick Answer

Use fine grits such as 1500 to 3000 with very light pressure where wet sanding is appropriate. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Best Grit Path

1500 → 3000

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Why It Happens

Edges have less margin for sanding and are easy to overwork with pressure.

Wet or Dry

Wet for fine clear coat sanding.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not expect sanding alone to create full gloss.
Success Check

The surface looks and feels even before the next step.
