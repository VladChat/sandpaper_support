Problem

Runs or drips are hard to level

Paint or clear coat runs remain raised after sanding.

Quick Answer

Use 800 to 1500 depending on the coating and defect height, then refine finer. Clean the surface and the sheet.

Best Grit Path

800 → 1500

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Why It Happens

A raised run needs careful leveling before fine refinement.

Wet or Dry

Wet for fine coating work.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
Success Check

The surface looks and feels even before the next step.
