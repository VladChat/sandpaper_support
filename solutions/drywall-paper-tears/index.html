Problem

Drywall paper tears while sanding

The drywall face paper tears or fuzzes during sanding.

drywall sheet handling 180 → 220 drywall paper tears while sanding the drywall face paper tears or fuzzes during sanding. drywall drywall face paper tears or fuzzes during sanding how to fix drywall paper tears while sanding paper tears early
Answer

Use 180 to 220 with very light pressure for patch smoothing. Let the patch dry fully.

Why it happens
The grit is too coarse, pressure is too high, or the sheet is catching the paper edge.
Recommended grit
Use 180 to 220 with very light pressure for patch smoothing.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

What to do

  1. Let the patch dry fully.
  2. Sand the high area with light pressure.
  3. Feather the edge into the surrounding surface.
  4. Remove dust before painting.
Avoid: Do not compensate for loading or wear with heavy pressure.
