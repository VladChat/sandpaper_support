Problem

What grit for cherry wood before finish?

Cherry can show sanding scratches and uneven color after finish.

wood dry sanding scratches medium grit
Answer

For cherry wood before finish, use 180 then 220. Use 320 only lightly if needed.

Why it happens
Cherry is fine-grained and can reveal uneven sanding under oil, stain, or clear finish.
Recommended grit
Use 180, then 220 before finish. Use 320 only for a light final touch if needed.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The cherry has an even scratch pattern and no visible sanding lines.

What to do

  1. Sand evenly with 180.
  2. Refine with 220.
  3. Inspect under good light for cross-grain scratches.
  4. Remove dust before finish.
Avoid: Do not leave random orbit or cross-grain marks before finish.
