Problem

Dust hides scratches until finish

Scratches appear only after dust is wiped away or finish is applied.

Quick Answer

Return to the grit that removes the visible scratches, then refine to the final prep grit. Clean the surface.

Best Grit Path

120 → 180 → 220 → 320

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Clean the surface.
  2. Use the recommended grit with light, even pressure.
  3. Inspect before changing grits.
  4. Move finer only after the old marks are gone.
Why It Happens

Sanding dust can fill scratches and make the surface look smoother than it is.

Wet or Dry

Dry.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not skip grit steps while visible scratches or high spots remain.
Success Check

The old marks are replaced by one uniform finer scratch pattern.
