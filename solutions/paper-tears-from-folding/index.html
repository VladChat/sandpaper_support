Paper tears at the fold

The sheet splits where it was folded.

Solution

Problem
The sheet splits where it was folded.
Likely cause
A hard crease concentrates stress in one line.
Recommended grit
Any grit can tear if sharply creased.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on the task.
Success check
The sheet stays intact through normal strokes.

Steps

  1. Fold gently without crushing the backing.
  2. Use a smaller piece if needed.
  3. Move the fold location as the abrasive wears.
  4. Use a block on flat areas.
Avoid: Do not create a tight crease and pull hard under pressure.
