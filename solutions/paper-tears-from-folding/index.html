Problem

Paper tears at the fold

The sheet splits where it was folded.

Quick Answer

Any grit can tear if sharply creased. Fold gently without crushing the backing.

Best Grit Path

120 → 180 → 220 → 320

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Fold gently without crushing the backing.
  2. Use a smaller piece if needed.
  3. Move the fold location as the abrasive wears.
  4. Use a block on flat areas.
Why It Happens

A hard crease concentrates stress in one line.

Wet or Dry

Dry or wet depending on the task.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not create a tight crease.
  • Do not pull hard under pressure.
Success Check

The sheet stays intact through normal strokes.

Related Problems
  • Paper tears on sharp edges
  • Paper tears from heavy pressure
  • Grit comes off the sheet
