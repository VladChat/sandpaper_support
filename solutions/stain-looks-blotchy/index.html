Problem

Stain looks blotchy after sanding

Stain absorbs unevenly after sanding.

Quick Answer

Prepare evenly with 180 or 220, based on the project needs. Sand with the grain where possible.

Best Grit Path

180 → 220

Step-by-Step Fix
  1. Sand with the grain where possible.
  2. Use the grit that removes the visible issue.
  3. Step finer gradually.
  4. Remove dust and inspect under good light.
Why It Happens

Uneven sanding pressure or mixed scratch patterns can leave inconsistent surface preparation.

Wet or Dry

Dry.

Mistakes to Avoid
  • Do not skip grit steps while visible scratches or high spots remain.
Success Check

The surface looks and feels even before the next step.
