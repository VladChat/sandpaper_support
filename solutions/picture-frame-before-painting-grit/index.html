Problem

What grit for picture frames before painting?

Picture frames can show scratches or chipped edges after painting.

paint dry sanding haze scratches
Answer

For picture frames before painting, scuff with 220 and refine visible areas with 320.

Why it happens
Small profiles and decorative edges need light sanding that scuffs without flattening detail.
Recommended grit
Use 220 for scuffing and 320 for smoother visible areas.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
The frame is clean, dull, and ready for paint.

What to do

  1. Clean the frame first.
  2. Scuff with 220 grit.
  3. Use 320 on smooth visible faces.
  4. Remove dust from corners before paint.
Avoid: Do not flatten decorative details with heavy sanding.
