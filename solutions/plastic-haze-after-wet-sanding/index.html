Problem

Plastic haze after wet sanding

Plastic looks cloudy after fine wet sanding.

plastic wet sanding refinement 1500 → 2000 → 3000 plastic haze after wet sanding plastic looks cloudy after fine wet sanding. plastic plastic looks cloudy after fine wet sanding how to fix plastic haze after wet sanding wet sanding leaves haze
Answer

1500, 2000, and 3000 for polishing preparation. Use clean water.

Why it happens
The surface has been refined but still needs finer grits or polishing if gloss is desired.
Recommended grit
1500, 2000, and 3000 for polishing preparation.
Wet or dry
Wet.
Success check
The haze is fine, even, and ready for the next finish step.

What to do

  1. Use clean water.
  2. Use very light pressure.
  3. Refine with each finer grit.
  4. Inspect after wiping clean.
Avoid: Do not create heat with heavy pressure.
