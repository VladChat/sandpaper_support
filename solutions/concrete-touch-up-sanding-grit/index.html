Problem

Can I use sandpaper on concrete?

Small concrete spots or edges need smoothing by hand.

dry sanding pressure coarse grit
Answer

For small concrete touch-ups, use 60 or 80, then 120. Hand sheets are not for large concrete grinding.

Why it happens
Concrete is hard and abrasive, so hand sandpaper is best for small touch-ups, not heavy grinding.
Recommended grit
Use 60, 80, or 120 for small concrete touch-ups. Use finer grits only after the high spots are removed.
Wet or dry
Dry; use dust control and protection.
Success check
The small concrete spot is smoother and no longer has sharp high points.

What to do

  1. Clean loose debris.
  2. Use 60 or 80 for rough high spots.
  3. Move to 120 for smoother touch-up.
  4. Stop when the edge or spot is level enough for the task.
Avoid: Do not expect hand sandpaper to replace a grinder for large concrete areas.
