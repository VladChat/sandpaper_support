Sand through clear coat on corners

Clear coat is removed on corners during sanding.

Solution

Problem
Clear coat is removed on corners during sanding.
Likely cause
Corners and edges sand faster because pressure is concentrated there.
Recommended grit
Use fine wet sanding grits with minimal pressure near corners.
Wet or dry
Wet for fine clear coat sanding.
Success check
The surface looks and feels even before the next step.

Steps

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
