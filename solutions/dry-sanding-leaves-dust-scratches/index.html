Problem

Dust creates extra scratches

Random scratches appear while dry sanding.

general sandpaper use scratch removal dust creates extra scratches random scratches appear while dry sanding. general sandpaper use random scratches appear while dry sanding how to fix dust creates extra scratches scratches too deep
Answer

Repeat the current grit after cleaning, then move finer. Stop and wipe the surface.

Why it happens
Loose grit or debris is trapped under the paper.
Recommended grit
Repeat the current grit after cleaning, then move finer.
Wet or dry
Dry.
Success check
Random new scratches stop appearing.

What to do

  1. Stop and wipe the surface.
  2. Clean or replace the sheet.
  3. Resume with lighter pressure.
  4. Inspect before moving finer.
Avoid: Do not sand over loose debris.
