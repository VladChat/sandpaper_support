Aluminum clogs paper

Aluminum residue loads into the abrasive and sanding slows.

Solution

Problem
Aluminum residue loads into the abrasive and sanding slows.
Likely cause
Soft metal residue can pack into the sheet, especially with pressure or a worn piece.
Recommended grit
Use the grit needed for shaping or prep, often 180 to 400.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on the task and cleanup needs.
Success check
The abrasive cuts cleanly without packed residue.

Steps

  1. Clean the surface and the sheet.
  2. Use clean water and light pressure.
  3. Refine with the recommended grit sequence.
  4. Wipe and inspect before moving to the next step.
Avoid: Do not let dirty slurry or trapped grit stay under the sheet.
