Problem

Plastic still feels rough

Plastic remains rough or uneven after sanding.

plastic wet sanding scratches clogging
Answer

Use 320, then 400, then 600. Keep pressure light to avoid gouging or heat marks.

Why it happens
The surface needs gentler step-by-step refinement to avoid deep scratches.
Recommended grit
Use 320 or 400 for prep, then 600 and finer for a smoother finish.
Wet or dry
Wet for fine stages.
Success check
The surface feels even with a controlled fine scratch pattern.

What to do

  1. Clean the plastic.
  2. Use light pressure.
  3. Progress through finer grits.
  4. Rinse residue before each finer grit.
Avoid: Do not use heavy pressure that can gouge or heat the plastic.
