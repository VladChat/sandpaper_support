How To

Short practical sanding guidance for common customer tasks.

How to Choose the Right Grit

Start from the task: removal, prep, finish, or polishing preparation. Use the least aggressive grit that still solves the problem.

How to Sand Wet or Dry

Dry sanding is usually faster for removal and prep. Wet sanding helps reduce dust and improve fine finishing.

How to Move Through Grits

Each grit should remove the scratch pattern from the previous grit before moving finer.

How to Avoid Deep Scratches

Use light pressure, avoid starting too coarse, and move through grits gradually.
