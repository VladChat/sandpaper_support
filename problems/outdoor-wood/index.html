What grit for outdoor furniture refinishing?

Outdoor furniture can have weathered wood, old finish, rough fibers, or peeling paint. Recommended grit: Use 80 or 120 for weathered rough areas, then 180 and 220 for finish prep.

What grit for pressure-treated wood?

Pressure-treated wood can be rough, fuzzy, or splintered before finishing. Recommended grit: Use 80 or 120 for rough boards, then 180 for smoother prep.
