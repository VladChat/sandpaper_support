Swirl Marks Remain

Circular or curved marks remain visible after sanding or wet sanding.

clear coatpaintfine gritwet sandingpolishing prep

Final grit is too coarse for polishing prep

Likely cause
The final grit did not refine the surface enough, or the sanding pattern was inconsistent.
Recommended grit
Continue with finer grits such as 1000, 1500, 2000, and 3000 as needed for polishing preparation.
Wet or dry
Wet sanding for fine finishing and polishing preparation.
Success check
The surface has a uniform fine haze with no deeper circular marks.

What to do

  1. Clean the surface.
  2. Use light pressure with fine grit.
  3. Sand evenly with controlled strokes.
  4. Move to the next finer grit only after the previous marks are uniform.
  5. Prepare for polishing after the final fine grit.
Avoid: Do not press harder to remove swirl marks with a coarse grit.
