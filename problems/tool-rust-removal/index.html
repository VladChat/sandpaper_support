What grit removes surface rust from tools?

Hand tools have light surface rust or dark oxidation. Recommended grit: Use 220 or 320 for surface rust, then 400 or 600 for smoother finish.

What grit for rusty garden tools?

Garden tools can stay rusty or rough after cleaning. Recommended grit: Use 80 or 120 for heavy rust, then 180 or 220 for smoothing.
