What grit removes dried paint drips?

Dried paint drips leave raised bumps or ridges in the finish. Recommended grit: Use 320 or 400 to level small dried drips, then 600 for finer prep.

What grit fixes spray paint orange peel?

Spray paint can dry with a bumpy orange peel texture. Recommended grit: Use 1000 or 1500 for light orange peel, then 2000 and 3000 before polishing.
