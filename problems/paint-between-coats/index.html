Paint Between Coats

Browse 3 matching answers for paint between coats (surfaces: chalk paint, enamel paint, latex paint; tasks: between coats).

What grit between chalk paint coats?

Chalk paint can feel gritty or show brush marks between coats. Recommended grit: Use 320 or 400 lightly between fully dry coats.

What grit between enamel paint coats?

Enamel paint can show dust nibs or poor adhesion between coats if the surface is not lightly scuffed. Recommended grit: Use 400 or 600 lightly between cured enamel coats.

What grit between latex paint coats?

Latex paint can feel rough or show dust between coats. Recommended grit: Use 320 or 400 lightly after latex paint dries fully.
