What grit for cabinets before painting?

Cabinet paint can peel or show scratches when the surface is not prepared correctly. Recommended grit: Use 180 or 220 to scuff existing finish. Use 320 lightly between primer or paint coats.

Cabinet door edges sand through too fast

Cabinet door edges lose finish or expose bare spots faster than flat areas. Recommended grit: Use 220 for edge scuffing and 320 for light between-coat sanding.

What grit between cabinet primer coats?

Cabinet primer can feel rough, dusty, or uneven between coats. Recommended grit: Use 320 between primer coats. Use 400 before a finer topcoat.
