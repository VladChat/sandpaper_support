Can I wet sand wood?

Wet sanding bare wood can raise the grain and make the surface feel fuzzy. Recommended grit: Use dry sanding for bare wood: 120, 180, then 220. Use wet sanding only on cured finishes when the finish maker allows it.

Can wet/dry sandpaper be used without water?

Wet/dry sandpaper can confuse customers because the name includes wet and dry. Recommended grit: Use dry sanding for early shaping and wood prep. Use wet sanding for fine finishing, clear coat, plastic, resin, and polishing prep.
