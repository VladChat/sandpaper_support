What grit removes marker from wood?

Marker ink remains visible on wood after cleaning. Recommended grit: Use 180 only if the mark remains, then blend with 220 and 320.

What grit removes pencil marks from wood?

Pencil layout marks can remain visible after light sanding. Recommended grit: Use 180 or 220 for light pencil marks. Use 120 only if the mark is deep or the surface is rough.

What grit for water rings on wood?

Water rings or light stains may remain after surface cleaning. Recommended grit: Start with 220 for light surface marks. Use 180 only if the finish or stain needs stronger leveling.
