Painted trim feels rough between coats

Painted trim can feel gritty or rough after the first coat dries. Recommended grit: Use 320 for normal smoothing, or 400 for finer trim paint prep.

Latex paint feels rough after drying

Latex paint can dry with a rough or gritty feel. Recommended grit: Use 400 for light smoothing. Use 320 first only when the roughness is more noticeable.
