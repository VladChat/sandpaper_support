Paper Tears Early

The sheet rips, catches, or wears through before expected.

sharp edgecornerhand sandingtoo much pressurerough surface

Paper tears on sharp edges or corners

Likely cause
The sheet is catching on sharp corners, burrs, or rough raised edges.
Recommended grit
Use the correct grit for the surface, but reduce pressure around edges.
Wet or dry
Dry or wet depending on task; use lighter pressure near edges.
Success check
The sheet moves smoothly without catching or tearing.

What to do

  1. Inspect the edge before sanding.
  2. Reduce pressure near corners.
  3. Fold the sheet carefully or use a sanding block where suitable.
  4. Avoid pulling the sheet hard across sharp edges.
Avoid: Do not force the sheet into a sharp corner under heavy pressure.
