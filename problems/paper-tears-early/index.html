Paper Tears Early

The sheet rips, catches, or wears through before expected.

Sandpaper tears on sharp edges

The sandpaper tears when sanding a sharp edge or corner. Recommended grit: Use 120 or 180 to break a sharp edge, then refine with 220 and 320 when a smoother edge is needed.

Sandpaper tears from folding

The sandpaper tears where it is folded. Recommended grit: The grit is not the main issue. Use the grit required for the job, but handle the sheet without hard creases.

Sandpaper tears from heavy pressure

The sandpaper tears early when too much pressure is used. Recommended grit: Use the correct grit for the cut needed. Move coarser for removal, then refine with the next finer grits.
