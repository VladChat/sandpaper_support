What grit to feather paint edges?

Paint repair edges remain visible after sanding. Recommended grit: Use 220 or 320 to level the edge, then 400 or 600 for finer primer prep.

What grit to sand paint runs?

Paint runs or sags leave raised lines after the paint dries. Recommended grit: Use 600 to level small runs, then 800 or 1000 for finer blending before polishing or recoating.

What grit removes orange peel from spray paint?

Spray paint has orange peel texture after drying. Recommended grit: Use 1000 or 1500 for light orange peel, then 2000 and 3000 before polishing.

What grit fixes a paint tape edge?

A raised paint edge from masking tape can remain visible after the tape is removed. Recommended grit: Use 320 to feather the edge, then 400 or 600 for finer repaint prep.

What grit removes primer overspray?

Primer overspray can leave a rough dusty texture around the repair area. Recommended grit: Use 600 for light primer overspray on prepared surfaces. Use 400 first only for heavier texture before repainting.
