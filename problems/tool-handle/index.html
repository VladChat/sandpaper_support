What grit for sanding an axe handle?

An axe handle can feel rough, splintery, or slippery after finish wear. Recommended grit: Use 120 on rough spots, then 180 and 220 before oil.

What grit for sanding a shovel handle?

A shovel handle can feel rough, dirty, or splintery. Recommended grit: Use 120 for splinters, then 180 and 220 before oil or sealant.
