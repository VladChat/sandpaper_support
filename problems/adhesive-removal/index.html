What grit removes dried glue from wood?

Dried glue remains on wood and can block stain or finish. Recommended grit: Use 120 or 180 for dried glue, then 220 for final wood prep.

What grit removes super glue from plastic?

Dried super glue can leave a raised spot or white residue on plastic. Recommended grit: Use 600 for raised glue, then 800, 1000, and 1500 to refine.

Should I sand sticker residue?

Sticker residue can gum up sandpaper and scratch the surface underneath. Recommended grit: Remove residue with cleaning first. Use 400 or finer only after the adhesive is mostly gone.
