What grit before polyurethane?

Wood needs a smooth but not over-polished surface before polyurethane. Recommended grit: Use 180 then 220 before applying polyurethane. Between cured coats, use 320 or 400 lightly.

What grit between polyurethane coats?

Polyurethane coats can feel rough or show small dust nibs between coats. Recommended grit: Use 320 or 400 between cured polyurethane coats.

What grit for oak before staining?

Oak can show sanding scratches or uneven stain if the final grit is wrong. Recommended grit: Use 120 if leveling is needed, then 180 and 220 before staining.

What grit removes mill glaze from wood?
