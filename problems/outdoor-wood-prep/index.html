What grit after pressure washing a deck?

Deck boards can feel fuzzy or raised after pressure washing. Recommended grit: Use 80 or 120 for raised fibers, then 150 if a smoother stain-ready surface is needed.

Fence still has splinters after sanding

Fence boards can still feel splintery after light sanding. Recommended grit: Use 80 or 120 to remove splinters, then 150 for stain prep.
