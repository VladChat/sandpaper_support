Can I sand a quartz countertop?

Quartz countertop scratches or dull spots can get worse with normal sandpaper. Recommended grit: Do not start with coarse grit. For minor test areas only, use 2000 or 3000 wet and polish afterward.

Can I sand a granite countertop?

Granite countertop sanding can leave dull spots or uneven polish. Recommended grit: Avoid sanding polished granite. For minor test work, use only 2000 or 3000 wet in a hidden area.
