What grit should I use for orange peel?

The user wants to reduce orange peel texture in a suitable finish. Recommended grit: Use the finest grit that levels the texture, commonly 1000 or 1500, then refine with 2000 and 3000.

Dry sanding clear coat leaves scratches

Clear coat shows visible scratches after dry sanding. Recommended grit: Use clean wet sanding with 1000, 1500, 2000, and 3000 for clear coat refinement.
