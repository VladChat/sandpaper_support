Which grit should I use before polishing?

The user needs a final sanding grit before polishing a suitable surface. Recommended grit: Use 2000 or 3000 before polishing where the surface and finish allow.

2000 grit does not make the surface shiny

The surface looks smooth after 2000 grit but still looks dull or hazy. Recommended grit: Use 1500, 2000, then 3000 before polishing. After 3000, use the correct polish for the material.
