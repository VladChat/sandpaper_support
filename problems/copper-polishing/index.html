What grit for copper sink scratches?

Copper sink scratches can look bright, uneven, or mismatched after sanding. Recommended grit: Use 800 for light scratches, then 1000 and 1500 before polishing or letting patina return.

What grit removes oxidation from copper?

Copper can look dull, stained, or uneven after oxidation. Recommended grit: Use 600 for light oxidation, then 800, 1000, and 1500 before polishing.
