What grit removes clear coat overspray?

Clear coat overspray can leave a rough texture on the surface. Recommended grit: Use 1500 or 2000 wet sanding for light overspray, then 3000 before polishing.

What grit for clear coat blend edge?

A clear coat blend edge can show a ridge or dull transition after repair. Recommended grit: Use 2000, then 3000 before polishing.
