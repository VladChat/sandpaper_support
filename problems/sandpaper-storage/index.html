How to store sandpaper sheets

Sandpaper sheets curl, bend, or feel uneven before use. Recommended grit: Store sheets flat, dry, and sealed when possible.

Does sandpaper expire?

Old sandpaper may feel weak, curled, or less effective. Recommended grit: Use the same grit if the sheet is clean, flat, and still cuts. Replace old sheets that are curled, contaminated, or worn.
