What grit for plastic bumper before primer?

Primer may not stick well to a plastic bumper if the surface is glossy or scratched too deeply. Recommended grit: Use 400 or 600 before primer. Use 320 only for rough repair areas first.

What grit for motorcycle fairings before paint?

Motorcycle fairings can show scratches or paint adhesion problems after sanding. Recommended grit: Use 400 or 600 for paint prep. Use 320 only for rough repaired spots.
