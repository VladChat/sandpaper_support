What grit for brass hardware before polishing?

Brass hardware may stay dull or scratched after cleaning. Recommended grit: Use 600 for oxidation or scratches, then 800, 1000, and 1500 before polishing.

What grit for brass polishing prep?

Brass can stay scratched or dull after sanding. Recommended grit: Use 600, 800, 1000, 1500, and 2000 before polishing.
