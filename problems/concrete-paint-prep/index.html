What grit for concrete patch before painting?

Concrete patch edges can show through paint if they are rough or raised. Recommended grit: Use 80 or 120 for leveling, then 180 or 220 for smoother paint prep.

What grit for peeling concrete paint?

Concrete paint can peel again if loose coating and rough edges remain. Recommended grit: Use 60 or 80 for heavy loose coating, then 120 for smoother edge blending.
