What grit to scuff powder coat before painting?

Paint may not stick well to a glossy powder-coated surface. Recommended grit: Use 320 or 400 to scuff powder coat before primer or paint.

What grit to scuff powder coated metal?

New paint may not bond well over slick powder coating. Recommended grit: Use 320 or 400 to scuff powder coat. Use 600 for finer visible topcoat prep.
