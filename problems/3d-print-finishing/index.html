What grit for resin 3D print support marks?

Resin 3D prints have small bumps or marks where supports were removed. Recommended grit: Use 400 or 600 for support marks, then 800, 1000, and 1500 for finer smoothing.

What grit for resin 3D prints before painting?

Resin 3D prints can show support marks or primer scratches after painting. Recommended grit: Use 320 or 400 for support marks, then 600 before primer.

What grit for PETG 3D prints?

PETG prints can gum up, smear, or keep visible layer lines during sanding. Recommended grit: Use 220 or 320 for layer lines, then 400 and 600 before primer.
