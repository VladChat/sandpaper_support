What grit between epoxy floor coats?

A new epoxy floor coat may not bond well over a glossy cured coat. Recommended grit: Use 120 or 180 to scuff cured epoxy, then clean thoroughly before recoating.

What grit to smooth epoxy floor flakes?

Decorative floor flakes can leave sharp or raised spots before topcoat. Recommended grit: Use 120 or 180 lightly to knock down flakes, then clean before clear coat.
