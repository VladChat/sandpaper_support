Can I sand carbon fiber?

Carbon fiber sanding can create harmful dust and can damage the weave or clear coat. Recommended grit: For clear coat only, use 1000, 1500, 2000, then 3000 wet before polishing.

Can I sand carbon fiber clear coat?

Carbon fiber parts can be damaged if sanding cuts through the clear coat. Recommended grit: Use 1000, 1500, 2000, and 3000 only on the clear coat before polishing.
