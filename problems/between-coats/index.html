What grit between shellac coats?

Shellac can feel rough or show dust nibs between coats. Recommended grit: Use 320 or 400 very lightly between fully dry shellac coats.

What grit to scuff sand sanding sealer?

Sanding sealer can feel rough or uneven before the final finish. Recommended grit: Use 320 or 400 after the sealer is dry.
