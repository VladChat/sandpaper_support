Surface Still Feels Rough

The surface does not become smooth even after sanding.

woodmetalplasticwrong grit sequencenot enough sanding

The surface needs gradual grit progression

Likely cause
The surface was not fully leveled at the current grit before moving finer, or grit steps were skipped.
Recommended grit
Use the current working grit until the surface is consistent, then step up gradually.
Wet or dry
Dry for prep; wet for fine finishing when appropriate.
Success check
The surface feels even before moving to the next grit.

What to do

  1. Return to the grit that still cuts the roughness.
  2. Sand until the surface feels consistent.
  3. Clean the surface.
  4. Move to the next finer grit.
  5. Repeat until the desired smoothness is reached.
Avoid: Do not move to very fine grit before the roughness is removed.
