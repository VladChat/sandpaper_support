Can sandpaper sharpen a knife?

Knife edges can be refined with sandpaper, but the angle must stay controlled. Recommended grit: Use 400 or 600 for edge work, then 1000, 2000, or 3000 for finer refinement.

What grit for lawn mower blade touch-up?

A lawn mower blade has burrs, rust, or rough edges after sharpening. Recommended grit: Use 80 or 120 for rust and burr cleanup, then 180 or 220 for smoother edge cleanup.

Can sandpaper sharpen chisels?

A chisel edge is dull and needs sharpening or edge refinement. Recommended grit: Use 400 or 600 for edge repair, then 1000, 1500, and 2000 for sharpening refinement.
