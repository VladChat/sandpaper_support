What grit for sanding plaster walls?

Plaster walls can show scratches or uneven repair edges after sanding. Recommended grit: Use 120 or 150 for leveling hard repair areas, then 180 or 220 before primer.

What grit to sand paint drips on a wall?

Paint drips on a wall can leave raised ridges after drying. Recommended grit: Use 220 to level dried drips, then 320 for smoother repaint prep.

What grit for spackle pinholes?

Small spackle repairs can leave bumps or visible edges under paint. Recommended grit: Use 220 for smoothing and 320 for feathering the edges.
