What grit between lacquer coats?

Lacquer can show dust nibs, uneven texture, or adhesion issues between coats. Recommended grit: Use 400 or 600 lightly between cured lacquer coats.

What grit between water-based finish coats?

Water-based finish can feel rough after the first coat. Recommended grit: Use 320 or 400 lightly between cured water-based finish coats.
