Browse 2 matching answers for primer prep (surfaces: primer sealer, oil-based primer; tasks: paint prep, primer sanding).

What grit for sanding primer sealer?

Primer sealer feels rough or shows dust before paint. Recommended grit: Use 400 or 600 lightly before paint.

What grit to sand oil-based primer?

Oil-based primer can show brush marks, dust nibs, or roughness before paint. Recommended grit: Use 320 or 400 after the primer is fully cured. Use 600 for finer topcoat prep.
