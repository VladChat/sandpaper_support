The user needs a grit for removing old paint. Recommended grit: Use 80 or 120 for removal, then 180 and 220 for prep.

What grit removes paint from a metal chair?

Old paint on a metal chair remains uneven or flaky before repainting. Recommended grit: Use 80 or 120 for loose paint removal, then 180, 220, and 320 before primer.
