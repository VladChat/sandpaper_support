Not Sure What Grit To Use

The customer does not know which grit to start with or which grit comes next.

remove materialprepare surfacebetween coatsfine finishpolishing prep

Choose the right starting grit

Likely cause
The task stage is not defined yet: removal, prep, finish, or polishing prep.
Recommended grit
60–120 for heavy removal; 150–240 for prep; 280–400 for finish prep; 600–800 for extra-fine finishing; 1000–3000 for polishing preparation.
Wet or dry
Dry for removal and prep; wet for fine finishing and polishing preparation.
Success check
The selected grit removes the defect without creating unnecessary deep scratches.

What to do

  1. Identify the task: remove, smooth, finish, or polish prep.
  2. Start with the least aggressive grit that can still do the job.
  3. Move finer gradually.
  4. Check the surface before each grit change.
Avoid: Do not start with 60 or 80 grit on a surface that only needs light smoothing.
