Not Sure What Grit To Use

The customer does not know which grit to start with or which grit comes next.

Skipped from 120 grit to 320 grit

Deep 120 grit scratches are still visible after switching to 320 grit. Recommended grit: After 120 grit, refine with 150 or 180, then 220, then 320.

Choosing a starting grit

It is unclear which grit to start with. Recommended grit: Use 60–120 for heavy removal, 150–240 for normal prep, 280–400 for fine prep, and 600–3000 for fine wet sanding or finishing stages.
