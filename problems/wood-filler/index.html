What grit for wood putty before paint?

Wood putty or filler can show as a raised patch under paint. Recommended grit: Use 180 or 220 to level filler, then 320 for paint prep.

What grit to sand wood filler flush?

Wood filler can stay raised or show scratches after sanding. Recommended grit: Use 120 or 180 to level filler, then 220 before finish.
