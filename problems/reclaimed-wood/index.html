What grit for pallet wood?

Pallet wood can be rough, splintery, and uneven before finishing. Recommended grit: Use 80 or 100 for rough cleanup, then 120, 180, and 220 for smoother prep.

What grit for barnwood?

Barnwood can be splintery, dirty, or too rough for finishing. Recommended grit: Use 80 or 100 for splinters, then 120 or 150 for a smoother rustic finish.
